Chicken Biriyani Recipe

Chicken biriyani is a classic aromatic rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken(bone-in pieces prefered)
  • 2/3 cup Plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • Basmati Rice

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Parboil the rice
  3. Prepare the base
  4. Layer the biriyani
  5. Dum cooking
  6. Rest and serve
